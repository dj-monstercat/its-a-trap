rules

To maintain a healthy community on plug.DJ, we want to make sure that the rules below are followed. This not only keeps our community running smoothly, but also keeps it friendly and clean for everyone. Please take the time to read the following rules carefully. The rules don't apply to dj-monstercat1

be active to play

If you would like to play a track and be part of the community you should chat with us. You are allowed to be inactive in the chat for 2 hours maximum, otherwise you will be booted out of the Waitlist, so please be aware of that.

No spamming

We don't want to be flooded with rubbish in the chat, especially when it's busy and active in the room. So think about what you send and keep the flooding to a minimum.

English only

We like to speak one language in our room which is the English Language. This is so we can all communicate together as one community, rather than not being able to understand each other.

Swearing

Yes, we do allow people to swear in the chat, and the tracks that get played can have swearing. The only condition is that nobody is allowed to swear with the intention to offend a particular group or person.

10 minute limit

To keep the wait times down and to prevent hour long tracks being played, maximum time for a track is 10 minutes. You can play a track over that, but it will automatically be skipped, so you have been warned.

overplay songs

To keep the room full of variety, we automatically skip tracks that are in the recent history, to keep it fresh and different, to prevent tracks becoming over played. Please refer to the blacklist and overplayed list to prevent being skipped. Blacklisted and overplayed songs are skipped with a boost to position #1 meaning the user gets another chance to pick another song.

No parodies

Please dont play parodies. They are not welcome in our community and so we have banned them to avoid conflict. If you want to play a song you found that was in a parody, please find the original version of the song on YouTube or Soundcloud and feel free to play it. (This includes Gun syncs, Minecraft parodies and videos from the NBF channel.)

about ranks

Please, do not ask to be promoted to staff. The only way for you to be promoted is when we personally ask you. You could fill in the bouncer application to get entered in our database so we know you are willing. Please don�t ask for staff in the room as it creates disturbance in the community.

Be nice

Please keep abusive and disrespectful comments to yourself. Keep the language down to a minimum, even though swearing is allowed, we want to keep it as clean and as friendly as possible in the community.

auto-joining

Firstly, we have DJ Cycle enabled in the community so therefore Auto-Join is fairly useless. We have an AFK check system in the community. Although we are not very strict on AFK checks, we do keep an eye for people who are AFK and in the waitlist. We do not allow Auto-Join whilst AFK because it increases the wait time to DJ in the waitlist. This is unfair for active users in the community.

use common sense

Just because something may not be on the rules, doesn't mean that you should do it. Use common sense.

about skipping

If a song is awful, and you want it to get skipped, then all you need to do is �Meh� the track to vote it off.

about moving

Asking to be moved up the list is not permitted but you can ask to be moved down the list. RDJs have priority in the WL meaning they are the only ones allowed to be moved up the list. We tend to give them a boost up the waitlist when they have released a new track.

about advertising

This is self-explanatory. You are not allowed to advertise other rooms or it may result in a ban.

about promoting

You may post self-promotion links twice when you are the DJ if and only if you are playing your own video or song.

Mislabeling

Mislabelling songs is not permitted and also intentionally mislabelling songs to make it seem like you made them will be instantly skipped and may result in a ban if carried on. This rule also applies to video titles that don't include the song title and artist.
